Exercise 2.13.

Show that under the assumption of small percentage tolerances there is a simple formula for the approximate percentage tolerance of the product of two intervals in terms of the tolerances of the factors. You may simplify the problem by assuming that all numbers are positive.

Ru: Русский текст упражнения

Scheme solution:

Assuming positive numbers:

Consider the two intervals: X ± a, Y ± b

i.e:

Center of first value

X

Center of second value

Y

Width of first value

a

Width of second value

b

Since all numbers are positive:

(define upper-bound-X (+ X a))

(define upper-bound-Y (+ Y b))

(define lower-bound-X (- X a))

(define lower-bound-Y (- Y b))

(define mult-value-upper-bound (* (+ X a) (+ Y b)))

(+ (* X Y) (* a Y) (* b X) (* a b))

(define mult-value-lower-bound (* (- X a) (- Y b)))

(+ (* X Y) (* (0 - a) Y) (* (0 - b) X) (* a b))

(define center-mult-value (/ (+ mult-value-upper-bound mult-value-lower-bound) 2))

(+ (* X Y) (* a b))

(define width-mult-value (/ (- mult-value-upper-bound mult-value-lower-bound) 2))

(+ (* a Y) (* b X))

(define percent-tolerance-mult-value (* (/ width-mult-value center-mult-value) 100))

(* (/ (+ (* a Y) (* b X)) (+ (* X Y) (* a b))) 100)

(* (/ (+ (* a Y) (* b X)) (+ (* X Y) (* a b))) 100)

If (* a b) is small with respect to (* X Y) it can be ignored giving:

(* (/ (+ (* a Y) (* b X)) (* X Y)) 100)

Which is

Toggle line numbers 
(* (+ (/ a X) (/ b Y)) 100)

i.e the approximate percentage tolerance can be expressed as:

(a/X + b/Y) * 100

e.g.

Tolerance of the interval got by multiplying 10 ± 0.1 and 20 ± 0.3 would be:

Toggle line numbers 
(* (/ (width (mul-interval (make-interval 9.9 10.1) (make-interval 19.7 20.3))) 
      (center (mul-interval (make-interval 9.9 10.1) (make-interval 19.7 20.3)))) 100)
;=> 2.4996250562415634

which is almost equal to - applying the formula (a/X + b/Y) * 100 :

Toggle line numbers 
(* (+ (/ 0.1 10) (/ 0.3 20)) 100)
;=> 2.5

немного по короче: если интервалы заданы как (make-center-percent c1 (* p1 100)) и (make-center-percent c2 (* p2 100)), то произведение [c1(1-p1);c1(1+p1)]*[c2(1-p2);c2(1+p2)] = [c1*c2*(1-(p1+p2)+p1*p2);c1*c2*(1+(p1+p2)+p1*p2)] ~= [c1*c2*(1-(p1+p2));c1*c2*(1+(p1+p2))]

Т.о. p* ~= p1+p2, а c* = c1*c2. Где результат равен (make-center-percent c* (* p* 100))
